NUTRITION

Maintaining a balanced and nutritious diet is essential for supporting overall health and vitality. A healthy diet should consist of a variety of nutrient-dense foods from all food groups, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Aim to consume plenty of colorful fruits and vegetables rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, and incorporate lean proteins such as poultry, fish, beans, and tofu into your meals. Limit your intake of processed foods, sugary snacks, and unhealthy fats, and opt for whole, unprocessed foods whenever possible. Stay hydrated by drinking an adequate amount of water throughout the day, and practice portion control to manage calorie intake effectively.
