DO's

- Do prioritize whole, unprocessed foods in your diet. - Do include a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. - Do drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated. - Do practice portion control to manage calorie intake. - Do establish a regular exercise routine to support overall health and fitness. - Do prioritize sleep and aim for 7 to 9 hours of quality sleep each night. - Do manage stress through relaxation techniques such as meditation, deep breathing, or yoga. - Do seek support from healthcare professionals for personalized nutrition and fitness advice.
