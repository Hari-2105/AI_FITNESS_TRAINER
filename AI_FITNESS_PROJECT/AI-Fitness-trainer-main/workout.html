Best Fitness Exercises

Pull-Up

Builds upper body strength by targeting back, shoulders, arms, and grip.

Push-Up

Strengthens the chest, shoulders, triceps, and core using just body weight.

Sit-Up

Targets abdominal muscles and enhances overall core strength and posture.

Walking

A simple yet powerful cardiovascular activity that boosts heart and mood.

Squat

Engages thighs, glutes, and core, enhancing balance and lower body power.

Jumping Jacks

Great for warming up and increasing endurance through full-body motion.

Lunges

Improves coordination and leg strength by targeting glutes and hamstrings.

Leg Raises

Strengthens lower abdominal muscles and enhances core stability and form.

Burpees

Combines squats, push-ups, and jumps for an intense full-body workout.
