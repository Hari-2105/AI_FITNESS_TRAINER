DONT's

- Don't rely on processed or convenience foods as staples in your diet. - Don't consume excessive amounts of sugary snacks, desserts, or beverages. - Don't skip meals or drastically restrict your calorie intake for weight loss. - Don't neglect the importance of regular physical activity for overall health. - Don't ignore signs of poor sleep quality or sleep disorders; seek help if needed. - Don't engage in crash diets or extreme weight loss methods that are unsustainable or unhealthy. - Don't let stress or negative emotions dictate your eating habits; practice mindful eating instead. - Don't hesitate to consult with a registered dietitian or nutritionist for personalized dietary guidance and support.
